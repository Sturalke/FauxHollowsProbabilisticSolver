Faux Hollows Solver

About

Attempt two at making a usable Faux Hollows minigame solver.
This time I hope I did it justice for anyone colorblind. Also focused on better browser compatibility.

The default settings are recommended for most people. Additional options are available in the advanced drop down menu. Most of this contains alternative strategies for focusing on particular game pieces, changing the coffer to a gift box, and more. Feel free to explore.
